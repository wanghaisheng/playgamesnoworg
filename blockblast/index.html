How to Play

  1. 1.

    Click adjacent blocks of the same color to select them. You need at least 3 matching blocks to make a valid move.

  2. 2.

    Selected blocks will disappear, and blocks above will fall down to fill empty spaces. New blocks will appear from the top.

  3. 3.

    Chain multiple matches together to create combos and earn bonus points. Higher combos mean higher scores!

  4. 4.

    Use power-ups strategically to clear difficult sections or boost your score multiplier.

Pro Tips

  • Look for opportunities to create large block clusters before making your move.

  • Pay attention to blocks above when clearing - plan ahead for cascading matches.

  • Save power-ups for when you're stuck or need to clear a large section quickly.

  • Practice timing your moves to maximize combo potential and score multipliers.
