Frequently Asked Questions

Can I watch Squid Game for free?

Squid Game is only available through a Netflix subscription. While Netflix occasionally offers free trials in some regions, you'll need an active subscription to watch the full series.

Is Squid Game available on other streaming platforms?

No, as a Netflix Original series, Squid Game is exclusively available on Netflix and cannot be found on other streaming platforms like Amazon Prime, Hulu, or Disney+.

What Netflix plan do I need to watch in 4K?

To watch Squid Game in 4K HDR quality, you'll need Netflix's Premium plan. The Standard plan offers HD quality, while the Basic plan provides standard definition streaming.

Are both seasons available worldwide?

Yes, both Season 1 and Season 2 of Squid Game are available to all Netflix subscribers worldwide. New episodes typically release simultaneously across all regions.
